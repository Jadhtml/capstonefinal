About Me

Hi my name is Jad, this is a page about me. I was born on June 30th 1999, in Ontario Canada. I have always been a creative person but I started creating edits using Capcut and Photoshop. I wanted to make a website where I can showcase my art and use it as a portfolio.

Inspiration

On social media, one of my favorite types of content are edits created with clips from anime, usually cut with music in the background. Seeing so many on TikTok and Instagram, I felt like this was something I could do too. So I downloaded Capcut and started my video editing journey there.

Hobbies

Some of my hobbies include painting, editing, working out, music, and dancing.
